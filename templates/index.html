How Does Tax Appealing Work?

Tax Appealing compares your home to similar properties in the same neighborhood. If your tax bill is too high, we'll help you file paperwork to decrease how much money you owe.

Why is Filing an Appeal Necessary?

Cook County's assessments of residential properties are infrequently updated and not uniform. An appeal asks the county to reassess your property and lower your tax bill.

Fix the Broken System

Cook County's property tax system is broken, but you don't need to hire an expensive lawyer to correct your bill. Tax Appealing makes it simple to pay your fair share of property taxes.
